HOSTING GUIDE

1. DEFINE YOUR STRATEGY

Before you go live, paste your topic or project details into the Strategy Analyzer. Click Generate to receive three viral-ready talking points that keep your audience from dropping off.

2. SYNC YOUR TIMELINE

When you start your Space, hit Start Space. The timer helps you manage your intro, deep-dive, and Q&A segments so you don't accidentally talk for too long on one topic.

3. MANAGE THE QUEUE

As speakers ask questions, type them into the Question Queue. This ensures you never have "dead air" and gives you a clear list of what to recap before the Space ends.

4. SAVE YOUR NOTES

Use the Saved Notes area at the bottom to jot down private thoughts or alpha during the session. These are saved locally so you can reference them for your post-Space thread.
